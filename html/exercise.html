    Trending Exercises

    Try out popular exercises chosen by most people

    Squat

    The squat is a fundamental compound exercise that targets multiple muscle groups in the lower body, primarily the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and lower back. It's often considered one of the most effective exercises for building lower body strength and functional fitness.

    Bench Press

    The bench press is a popular strength training exercise that primarily targets the muscles of the chest, shoulders, and triceps. It is typically performed lying on a flat bench, hence the name "bench press."
    Lat Pulldown

    The lat pulldown is a popular strength training exercise that targets the muscles of the upper back, particularly the latissimus dorsi, or "lats."


    Core Exercises

    Exercise that prioritize core strength

    Plank

    The plank is a core strengthening exercise that targets multiple muscles in the body, primarily focusing on the abdominals, obliques, lower back, shoulders, and chest. It's a static exercise, meaning you hold a specific position for a set amount of time rather than performing dynamic movements.
    Bicycle Crunches

    Bicycle crunches are a more dynamic variation of the traditional crunch, incorporating twisting and leg movement to engage both the upper and lower abdominal muscles, as well as the obliques.

    Russian Twist

    The Russian twist is a core exercise that primarily targets the obliques, the muscles on the sides of your abdomen, as well as the rectus abdominis (front abdominal muscles) and the lower back. It's a rotational movement that engages the core muscles to stabilize the torso while twisting from side to side.

    Leg Exercises

    Leg strengthening circuits

    Lunges

    Lunges are a versatile lower body exercise that primarily target the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes, while also engaging the calves and core muscles for stabilization. Lunges involve stepping forward or backward into a split stance and lowering your body until both knees are bent at roughly 90-degree angles.
    Leg Press

    The leg press is a resistance exercise performed on a leg press machine, primarily targeting the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. It's an effective exercise for building lower body strength and muscle mass while minimizing stress on the lower back.

    Calf Raise

    Calf raises are a simple yet effective exercise for targeting the calf muscles, specifically the gastrocnemius and soleus. The primary function of the calf muscles is plantar flexion, which is the movement of pointing your toes downward, as in standing on your tiptoes.


    Arm Exercises

    Arm strengthening circuits

    Bicep Curl

    The bicep curl is a fundamental exercise for targeting the biceps brachii muscle, located on the front of the upper arm. The bicep curl primarily works the biceps brachii muscle, along with assistance from the brachialis and brachioradialis muscles.
    Tricep Dip

    The tricep dip is an effective exercise for targeting the triceps brachii muscle, located on the back of the upper arm. Tricep dips primarily work the long head of the triceps muscle, along with assistance from the lateral and medial heads.

    Lateral Raise

    Lateral raises are an excellent exercise for targeting the deltoid muscle, specifically the lateral head, which is located on the side of the shoulder. Lateral raises primarily isolate the lateral deltoid, helping to improve shoulder width and definition.
