    • Roasted Squash & Beetroot Salad

    Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes

    This vibrant salad of roasted butternut squash and beetroot is topped with low-fat feta and roasted pepitas for some extra creaminess and crunch. Colorful, healthy and easy to make, it’s great as a side dish or a main meal!

    1. 200g butter squahs

    2. 500g beetroot

    3. 2tbsp olive oil

    4. 100g salad greens

    5. salt, to taste

    6. 50g dry-roasted pepitas

    7. 100g low-fat feta cheese

    8. 1/2 tsp garlic powder

    1.DPreheat oven to 200°C / 400°F.

    2. Peel and cut butternut squash and beetroot into 1 inch cubes.

    3. Scoop out about 2 tbsp of flesh from each avocado half to make more room for the egg.

    4. In a large serving bowl, lay a bed of salad greens, then top with roasted squash, beetroot, crumbled feta cheese and roasted pepitas. Give everything a good toss and enjoy!



    1.There’s no need for additional dressing or seasonings for the salad as feta cheese is already salty, and the oil and seasonings from the roasted squash and beetroot will season the rest of the salad when tossed.

    2.Feel free to substitute pepitas with any other dry-roasted nuts or seeds. Pinenuts, almonds, hazelnuts or this Chipotle Spiced Nuts will also work well.

    3.For vegan or dairy-free, feel free to substitute feta cheese with a plant-based alternative.
