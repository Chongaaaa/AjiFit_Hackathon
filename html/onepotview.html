    • One Pot Rice Cooker Fried Rice

    Prep Time: 15 minutes | Bake Time: 12 minutes

    Easy one pot “fried rice” that can be made in the rice cooker in under 30 minutes! While there is no “frying” involved, all the classic flavours you love in fried rice is there. Perfect for those lazy nights when you're craving take out!

    1. 1cup Raw Jasmine rice

    2. 240ml chicken broth

    3. 1cup frozen mixed vegetables

    4. 200g chicken thigh

    5. 1 egg

    6. 1 tsp light soy sauce

    7. 1/4tsp garlic powder

    Chicken Marinade

    1. 1tsp shao xing wine

    2. 1 tsp sesame oil

    3. 2 tsp light soy sauce

    1. Cut up chicken into 0.5 inch cubes. Add to a medium bowl with all the chicken marinade ingredients. Mix well. Cover and set aside while you prepare the other ingredients.

    2. Crack egg into a small bowl and lightly beat. Set aside.

    3. Wash rice a few times until the water runs clear. Then drain very well using a sieve.

    4. Add rice, chicken broth, fish sauce, light soy sauce, garlic powder and onion powder to rice cooker pot. Stir to mix well.

    5. Add chicken, vegetables and beaten egg. Give everything a brief stir.

    6. Close the lid and set the rice cooker to cook.

    7. When done, mix everything together gently using a rice spatula. Serve immediately. Enjoy!

    1.This recipe is very versatile and you can swap out most ingredients as long as you keep the dry to wet ratio around the same.

    2. You may substitute chicken stock with vegetable stock, or 240ml water + 1 cube chicken bouillon.
